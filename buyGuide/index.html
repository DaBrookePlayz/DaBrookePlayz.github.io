Imporant thing to note

For the vast majority of computer users, you DO NOT need to build your own PC. If you do not feel comfortable dealing with computer parts, then just buy a pre-built computer, or even a laptop. While pre-built towers tend to be more expensive than just building it on your own, one can easily break components or get electrocuted by improperly building a PC. Custom-built PC sare also typically meant for those who need a very high performance computer with expensive parts.
